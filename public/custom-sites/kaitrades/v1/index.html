About KaiTrades

A trader-first brand built around clarity.

KaiTrades is a permanent acceptance-test academy that demonstrates a serious route into forex: risk management, journal discipline, technical analysis, and direct access to a community that studies the market every week.

The goal is not noisy hype. It is a repeatable process that helps each client understand why a setup matters before they take the trade.
