XM broker readiness

A verified XM account is required to participate in the mentorship programme.

How to open your XM account

  1. Visit XM using the mentor's referral link — check your welcome message or the XM Setup guide on this site.
  2. Click Open Live Account and complete the registration form with your personal details.
  3. Upload your ID document and proof of address for KYC verification.
  4. Wait for account approval — typically 1 – 2 business days.
  5. Make your first deposit and ensure the account is funded before your first session.

Your mentor will share the exact referral link, recommended account type, and minimum deposit in your welcome message.
