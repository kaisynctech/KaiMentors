Account opening steps

  1. Register: Click the approved broker link and complete your personal details.
  2. Verify: Upload your ID and proof of address inside the broker client area.
  3. Select account: Choose the account type recommended during mentorship.
  4. Fund carefully: Start with capital you can manage with strict risk rules.
  5. Send proof: Share your broker account ID with the KaiTrades support team.
