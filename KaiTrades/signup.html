Broker readiness

A funded broker account is required to participate in the mentorship programme.

How to open your broker account

  1. Visit the broker's official website using the link provided by your mentor.
  2. Click Open Live Account and complete the registration form with your personal details.
  3. Upload your ID document and proof of address for verification.
  4. Wait for account approval — this usually takes 1 – 2 business days.
  5. Make your first deposit and ensure the account is funded before your first session.

Your mentor will share the exact broker link, recommended account type, and minimum deposit in your welcome message. For more detail, see the Broker Setup guide.
