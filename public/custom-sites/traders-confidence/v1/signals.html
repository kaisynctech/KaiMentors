The MD415 Method

Top-down. In four layers.

A repeatable way to read any chart: zoom from what the market is doing, to where it will react, to how you get in.

01
Bias Layer — direction first

What is the market really doing?

Every decision starts with one question: up or down? You learn to read the dominant structure on the chart and commit to trading only in its direction — so you stop fighting the market.

The exact read is taught one-on-one
02
Level Layer — where price reacts

Where is price likely to turn?

Direction alone isn't enough — timing needs location. You learn to identify the high-probability zones where the market tends to react, and the discipline to wait for price to reach them.

The zones we use are covered in your sessions
03
Entry Layer — how you get in

When do you actually pull the trigger?

This is where bias and level come together into a precise, rules-based entry with clearly defined risk. The full trigger — and exactly how to manage the trade — is taught step by step with Bongani.

Full entry rules unlocked when you train with Bongani

Core principle

Big picture→Key level→Trigger

The whole method moves from "what" to "where" to "how" — a calm, top-down routine instead of chasing every candle. The detail behind each step is exactly what you learn in a session.
