How a session works

Simple, personal, practical.

  1. Tell Bongani where you are

    Complete and beginner, or already trading — you share your level and what you want to achieve.

  2. Pick your format

    Face-to-face or online, at a time that works for you.

  3. Learn on live charts

    Bongani teaches the MD415 method applied to real, current market conditions — and to your own trades.

  4. Leave with a plan

    You walk away knowing what to practise next, with a clear, repeatable process to follow.
