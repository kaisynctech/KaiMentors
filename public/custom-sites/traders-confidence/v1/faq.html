No. Whether you've never placed a trade or you're already trading, sessions begin from where you are and build up at your own pace.

Why do I need to sign up with XM?

Traders Confidence is an official XM Global partner. Opening your account with code MD415 links you to us — that's how your portal access is verified and how Bongani is able to support you directly.

What's inside the student portal?

Once you're verified you get downloadable resources and PDFs, a growing library of video tutorials, the full MD415 method, and the ability to book one-on-one sessions with Bongani — all in one place.

Both. You can sit down face-to-face or join a live online session from anywhere in the world — you get the same personal attention either way.

What will I actually learn?

The MD415 top-down method — how to read direction (bias), find the right levels, and time entries with defined risk — taught step by step around what you need next.

No. Everything offered by Traders Confidence is strictly educational and is not financial, investment or trading advice. Trading carries significant risk, and any trade or account you open is entirely at your own risk.
